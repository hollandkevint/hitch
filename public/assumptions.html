B2B2C data-product assumptions

Couple usage has to create planner business value.

The hard part is not whether an assistant can draft advice. The hard part is whether a planner-buyer will trust an assistant acting inside a shared wedding record used by couples, planners, and vendor-facing workflows.

  • Buyer/user split: planners buy capacity; the couple's daily usage keeps the record fresh enough to act on.
  • Governed record: the record must be fresh enough to ground actions and structured enough to audit writes.
  • Trust loop: write approval, edit rate, reversal rate, and planner corrections determine whether autonomy can expand.
